Reading

Read through Part I: Data Exploration (Chapters 2 - 8). This looks like a lot of material, and it is, but your primary goal is only to familiarize yourself with what topics are covered where in the text. Pay particular attention to Chapter 3 which introduces ggplot2 (which may be familiar to many of you) and Chapter 5, which introduces five common dplyr functions we’ll be using. Chapters 4, 6, and 8 cover are short but excellent sections on some basics of workflow which we’ll be following throughout the course.
